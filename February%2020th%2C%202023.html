Drove to Bowen today to drop off Rachael’s art at Hearth Gallery for a group show she is in.

At the ferry, we got some vending machine snacks, including Spinnakers Blackberry Gingerale. Very spicy ginger bite, and great to see BC Ferries stocking local products.

My mom made some lunch for us — some pasta and tomato sauce and meatballs and salad. I had some red wine with lunch — opened a bottle of Argentinian red Escorihuela Gascón 1884 Cabernet Sauvignon 2019

Back home, I opened a gift from Chad, a bottle of Ikikko Deluxe Mugi Shochu. Chad spent time on the island of Iki, and this is Mugi Shochu aka Barley Shochu, but barrel aged:

Ikikko Deluxe Mugi Shochu

Barley shochu from Iki

Original Shochu is matured for long time in old sherry barrel, which is imported from Spain, and it becomes ruby colored Shochu. Smooth texture and rich taste.
