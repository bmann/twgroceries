Mi Tierra Latina

GroceryLatin AmericanThe DriveWest End

A Latin American grocery store.

In early 2020, Sonia, Nilka, Michelle and Alexandra realized that a key element missing from Vancouver’s West End was a fully stocked grocery filled with Latin American goodies. More often than not, it is not that easy to go out of Vancouver to buy specific products, and it is not always possible to fly out to handpick delectable items to bring home.

The all-women team then set out to open Mi Tierra Latina, they have made it a point to fill their shelves with every product they could get their hands on from various Latin American countries
