Neapolitan Semolina Cake

Boris Mann 29th August 2021 at 7:40am
cakeCook's Illustrated July & August 2021ItalianRecipericottasemolina

aka Migliaccio Di Semolino - Semolina and Ricotta Cake

  • 4 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 12 ounces / 1 1/2 cups whole-milk ricotta cheese
  • 2 Tbsp orange liquer
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup semolina flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 2 Tbsp grated lemon zest (2 lemons)
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp grated orange zest
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp table salt
  • confectioner's sugar for dusting

Made using Bob's Red Mill Semolina Flour
