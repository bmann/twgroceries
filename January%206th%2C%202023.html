Journal

Made myself a big lunch at home.

Diced up some bacon ends and sautéed them. Added half a red onion. Chopped lacinto kale.

Pepper sprayed myself by adding ancho chile powder while the pan was quite hot.

Added chicken stock and some soft goat cheese and reduced.

Added in some eggs and grated cheddar on top and put it under the broiler.

For dinner, finished up some green beans with bacon, onions, and garlic, plus a little lemon juice and Trader Joes Agrodolce. Plus bratwurst.
