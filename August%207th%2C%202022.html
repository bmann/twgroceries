Went to Chambar for a nice breakfast with friends, continuing the Rachael birthday celebrations.

I had a new dish on the menu, the Roti du Boeuf.

Wandered around downtown and did some shopping at The Bay, Vans, Winners, Daiso, and Golden Age Collectables. Then checked out the brand new Rainbow Park which has a Kafkas in it. A quick stop at the Contemporary Art Gallery.

Made some quick pickled radishes —

Quick Pickled Radishes

  • 10 radishes, thinly sliced
  • ⅔ cup white wine vinegar
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 star anise pod
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seed
  • ½ teaspoon black peppercorns

No star anise, added dried dill seeds, and some sliced shallots.

Used regular white vinegar and added some Trader Joes Agrodolce.

Also put some pickles brining with the Kraut Source Fermented Pickles recipe.

Used shallots instead of onion, dried minced garlic chips instead of fresh cloves, and didn’t add any tea leaves.

I also had a much smaller jar so stood up some pickles but also sliced some to fit in on top.

And now I’m pulling out Joy of Pickling, looking up Refrigerator Pickles, and browsing what to do with hot banana peppers I bought yesterday.
