It’s been a bit of a whirlwind couple of days.

Yesterday December 14th, 2022 went to Como Taperia for Fission holiday dinner.

Today at lunch went out to run some errands at Yama Cafe. It’s a Japanese cafe that also has lots of little products for sale there, including bookmarks that Rachael likes to give as small gifts.

They have some Japanese traditional Christmas time things to pre-order right now, like Strawberry Short Cake and Strawberry Roll Cake.

The Yamas Special Osechi (Osechi is a Japanese traditional New Year's dish. Many kinds of Japanese traditional foods are packed in three tiered Jubako) is $380, so way too much for me to just buy on a whim — but lots of things for me to research and I’d love to try it.

I got take out porchetta sandwich and curried chickpea yam soup from Edge Kitchen, which is in the old Freybe space on East Hastings just east of Victoria Drive. Turns out it’s been there for 3 years and is also the main space for Edge Catering that also runs a storefront cafe with breakfast and lunch and a few local products for sale.

I can’t tell you about the food because I didn’t eat it yet, but instead went up the Drive to meet my mom at Livia for lunch instead. Had the spaghetti carbonara.

Finally, went for dinner with Bentzen to Do Chay on Kingsway. A vegetarian “upscale” Vietnamese place. It was VERY good. And also a Barb & Roland recommendation.
