Aviation Cocktail

Boris Mann 27th July 2023 at 4:24am
cocktailIBAUnforgettables
  • 45 ml gin
  • 15 ml maraschino Luxardo
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 1 barspoon Crème de violette

Preparation

Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Shake with cracked ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
