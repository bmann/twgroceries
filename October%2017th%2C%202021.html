I ate the last of the Thanksgiving 2021 turkey wings for lunch today.

Ended up going for a short walk when it wasn't raining too badly and going to Choices and Santa Barbara and getting a few more things.

Santa Barbara for apples and broccoli.

Choices for:

  • English muffins
  • cream
  • eggs
  • flax seeds
  • pumpkin seeds
  • carrots
  • oat milk
  • delicata squash
  • butter

And yes, turkey wings that I put right into the freezer and will make another time.

The beef fingers were in the sous vide all night without any sort of flavouring. This morning I put them in a bowl with a marinade of powdered onion, chili garlic sauce, soy sauce, salt, pepper, garlic granules, and hoisin sauce.

After chilling in the fridge, I put them in a 300F oven and am going to let them braise covered. Final step will be to run them under the broiler to crisp them up.
