Paris-Brest

Boris Mann 23rd February 2023 at 4:12pm
dessertFrenchpastry

This is a spectacular confection! Named for a bicycle race run between the two cities of Paris and Brest, this wheel-shaped pastry of choux paste is hands down impressive. The classic version is made with a praline-flavored center but this pastry cream version is just as tasty.
