Researched some options for my birthday dinner tomorrow. Forage, Straight and Marrow, Pidgin.

Ended up picking Collective Goods, French-ish place which is up on Commercial across from Flourist. It’s by the people behind Mackenzie Room and Say Mercy!.

Ended up sharing what is becoming a a repeatable Vancouver Recommendations for some out of town visitors,

Vancouver Recommendations

Miku is high end Japanese for lunch or dinner. Sushi, sake, and yes hot dishes as well.

Lin Chinese has good xiao long bao and just generally solid classic dishes.

Yu Xiang Yuan Restaurant is Blaine’s choice that I’ll happily adopt for great classic Chinese hand made noodles. Also great fluffy pan fried dumplings.

Mr Red Cafe for northern style Vietnamese.

Baby Dhal Roti is one of the few Caribbean, specifically Trinidadian, restaurants around.

Laksa King for top Singaporean dishes.
