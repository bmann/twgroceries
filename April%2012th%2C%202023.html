Great suet is dependent on what the cattle are being fed. That’s why we use suet from local farms that raise their cattle humanely and consistently on a clean diet.

People often wonder what gives the beef patty its distinct “cheddar cheese” colour. It’s not corn flour. It’s actually a mix of both turmeric and annatto
