Made Up Mapo Tofu

Boris Mann 14th September 2021 at 4:23am
mapo tofuMeal Prep

I didn't have any Szechuan peppercorn. I vaguely looked at some recipes, confirmed that I had very few of the "real" ingredients other than ground pork and tofu, so I made up the rest!

Here's a vague list of things I put in. Why yes, I do have a lot of condiments!

  • half an onion, diced
  • 1 Tbsp slivered fresh ginger
  • Sambal Oelek
  • hoisin sauce
  • soy sauce
  • soft tofu
  • sesame oil Lee Kum Kee Sesame Oil
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • half bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup shisito pepper, chopped
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • Datu Putu White Vinegar Spiced – Filipino spicy garlic vinegar
  • Aroy-D Sweet Chili Sauce for Chicken
  • 2 tsp fresh cracked green peppercorn

Ultimately, tasty! Some heat, some garlic, some vinegar, some sweet, and the green peppercorn actually added a nice base spice that worked out.

Comment from Rachael: more tofu! Apparently she'd like a 1:1 meat to tofu ratio.
