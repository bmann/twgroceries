Ebelskivers (Danish Pancakes) Recipe

Boris Mann 28th December 2022 at 8:40pm
AllTheBestRecipesDanishebelskiverspancakesRecipeSerious Eats
  • 1 cup flour,
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder,
  • 1/4 tsp salt,
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar,
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1 cup milk,
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup jam

This is our base Ebelskiver recipe we use. You can use any kind of jam. The Danes only eat this at Christmas time, but we have it for special brunches all the time. And, you can make them savoury as well.
