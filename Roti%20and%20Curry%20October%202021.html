Meal Prep

I used Dad’s Trinidadian Curried Chicken as the starting point for the chicken curry, which in turn starts with Green Seasoning.

I chopped off the tops of the drumsticks and removed the skin, and placed in a bowl with some salt and juice from two fresh limes.

Parsley, cilantro, and dandelion leaves all went into the blender with a whole fresh jalapeno, 6 cloves of garlic, juice from a fresh lime, and a couple of roma tomatoes to add liquid so I could actually blend it. This went into the chicken drumsticks to marinate for a couple of hours.
