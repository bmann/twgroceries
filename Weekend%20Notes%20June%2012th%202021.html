Friday

On Friday, a steak dinner with Katie & Brooke:

Some very nice steaks from Sebastian & Co Fine Meats – a rib eye and a t-bone to try different kinds. Rib eye continues to be my favourite.

I use the same Steak in a Pan Recipe all the time:

Use 1 Tbsp or so of vegetable oil, ideally in a cast iron pan. Turn to medium - medium high heat.

Put the steak in the pan and cook on one side for 2 minutes. Add butter and any aromatics or additional spices – e.g. garlic, fresh herbs.

Put in a pre-heated 300F oven for about 10min for medium - rare.

Let rest, then slice and finish with some more salt.

I sliced up tomatoes with olive oil on the side, plus also sautéed some fiddleheads with some butter and garlic scapes, plus splashed on some sherry vinegar on.

Actually my first time cooking fiddleheads. Like most greens, butter and garlic and salt went well with them.
