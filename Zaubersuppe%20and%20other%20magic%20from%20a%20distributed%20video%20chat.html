Franzi made a "Zaubersuppe" – a magical, purple soup, because that's what happens when you add purple carrots:

https://twitter.com/_franzihei/status/1242193973994590208?s=20

Franzi also explained that she was from the extreme west of Germany, from an area called Westphalian (Westfalen auf Deutsch) ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westphalia)).

And that there were a set of dishes called "Untereinander", which is mashed potatoes mixed with other things – roughly, "mixed together", in English. Her favourite is "Endivien Untereinander", here's a version I found:

https://www.rezeptwelt.de/sonstige-hauptgerichte-rezepte/endivien-untereinander-rheinland/xyigv0ys-d2797-942160-cfcd2-a6xsxb6k
