Drinks at MILA September 5th, 2021

Boris Mann 6th September 2021 at 9:57pm
JournalMILA

First plan was to go to Boxcar, but it was closed for a private event.

We ended up at the outdoor patio of MILA. It's a vegan restaurant, so everything is plant based. We tried the MILA Ocean Roll (cured watermelon instead of tuna), the MILA Fish Burger (battered celeriac), and MILA Mixed Vegetable and Chickpea Pakoras.

It was happy hour so I ended up trying the MILA Isla Nubar cocktail.
