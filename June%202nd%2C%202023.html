June 2nd, 2023

Boris Mann 3rd June 2023 at 8:35pm
Journal

Drove back from Galiano Island on the 8:30am ferry. Went to the Sturdies Bay Café & Bakery for breakfast — sausage & egg on an English muffin and a cappuccino.

Dropped everybody off and then went to drop off the rental van. Got a cappuccino at Lucky’s Doughnuts where the Enterprise rent-a-car is on Thurlow.

Dinner at Jagerhof Restaurant in North Van for my dad’s 86th birthday. I got the platter with three meats: a mini schnitzel, bratwurst, and kassler. Served with braised sauerkraut, and I got spaetzle instead of potatoes. Had a Stiegl Grapefruit Radler. My mom brought her Donau Welle cake.
