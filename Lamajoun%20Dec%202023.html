Lamajoun Dec 2023

Boris Mann 31st December 2023 at 8:06am
LamajounMeal Log

The menu hasn’t changed, but the layout has. There’s a small ordering / pickup area, and now you can go upstairs to the second floor and sit down. Many more tables than there were before.

We got two khachapuri “bread boats”, a salad, and pickled vegetables, and it was delicious.

You order and pay downstairs and get a number. Then when your food is ready it comes up in a dumb waiter and announces your number. So fun!

Here we are leaving but already making plans to come back again! Adrian holding some white mulberry jam he bought to try.
