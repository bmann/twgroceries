Before heading home from the coworking space, I went to Les Amis du Fromage and picked up some nice cheeses:

  • Grey Owl Cheese, Quebec
  • Appenzeller Classic, Switzerland
  • Tomme du Haut-Richelieu Cheese, Quebec
  • Madelaine Cheese, Quebec
  • Queso de Cabra al Romero Cheese, Spain

Got take out from Nonna’s Cucina Italian Street Food and ate it in Woodland Park in the sun. Our order:

  • pappardelle with peas, asparagus, and truffle cream
  • vegetarian panini with fig jam: really great bread and not too bready, and the sweetness from the fig jam was nice
  • panzanella aka salad with bread: vinaigrette dressing needed a little more
  • a side of two meatballs: sauce was a little flat and the meatballs needed a bit more salt

Along with some tepache: this edition has ginger, a cinnamon stick, and then I added orange juice from 4 oranges I needed to use up.

The cinnamon stick flavour didn’t really come through. The fresh orange is really nice. We’ll see how the two bottles that are fermenting turn out.
