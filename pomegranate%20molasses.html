Pomegranate molasses, also known as Dibs Ar-rumman (Arabic: دِبْس الرُّمَّان, rakkaz rimonim Hebrew: רכז רימונים, robb-e anâr Persian: رب انار, melása rodioú Greek: μελάσα ροδιού, "pomegranate syrup"), and nar ekshisi (Turkish: nar ekşisi, "pomegranate sour"), is a Middle Eastern seasoning consisting of concentrated pomegranate juice. It is usually used in fish and meat dishes, and also as a replacement for vinegar in salads.
