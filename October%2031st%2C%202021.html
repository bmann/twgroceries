Yesterday started with a noon visit to The Pie Shoppe which was hosting a pop up by another baker, Dessert Club.

We got the Apple Cardomom Cake and the Pistachio Cream Pudding, and also bought some raspberry plum jam.

Then we drove out to the Richmond Art Gallery.

We looked around on the map where to eat and went over to John 3:16 Malaysian Food but it was take out only.

A little further we found Kumare, a Filipino bakery and restaurant.

Rachael had the lomi and I had the Kumare Longsilog All Day Breakfast, plus we decided to try the Kumare Deep Fried Tofu.

At the till while paying, we bought a slice of cassava cake and some purple ube muffins.

From there we drove to Wild Sweets.

The final stop was the Richmond Farm Market.
