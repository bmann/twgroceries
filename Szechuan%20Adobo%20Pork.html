Szechuan Adobo Pork

Boris Mann 8th June 2023 at 6:56am
garlicMeal LogporkSichuan

I bought a relatively small pork picnic shoulder the other day Donalds Shopping June 4th, 2023 and made it for dinner tonight.

I put the pork in a bowl to marinade a bit before cooking. I had a bunch of garlic cloves that I bought a while back that needed using up so I added about 30 of them or so.

  • 30 garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 Facing heaven pepper, dried and crumbled in with seeds
  • 2 Tbsp Szechuan peppercorns
  • 2 Tbsp fresh minced ginger
  • 2 diced shallots
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 3 peeled and chopped carrots

I let this sit for a couple of hours (prepped at lunch) and stirred it to cover several times.

Put it in an oven proof pot and cooked at 350F covered for a couple of hours. Took it out, added 2 cups of water and added 5 red potatoes, quartered, and cooked for another hour.

It was spicy and salty and garlicky. Adobo would normally have a vinegar element and Bay leaves but I forgot both of them.

The pork was tender and the potatoes had soaked up the dark soy and the spice.
