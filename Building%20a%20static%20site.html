You load it and then build it.

tiddlywiki --load twgroceries.html --build static --verbose

Helpful links:

  • https://talk.tiddlywiki.org/t/easy-method-of-setting-up-a-static-site/3820/6
  • https://github.com/saqimtiaz/TW5-github-actions-example
