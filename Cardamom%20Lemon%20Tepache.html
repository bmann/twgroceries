Cardamom Lemon Tepache

Boris Mann 18th April 2021 at 3:38pm
home madeMeal Preptepache

I just burped the three bottles of tepache I’m making — first of the season.

Take a large container with de-chlorinated water (you can leave water out overnight) and add all the ingredients.

  • juice of 3 lemons
  • skin and core of one pineapple
  • 6 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cup cane sugar
