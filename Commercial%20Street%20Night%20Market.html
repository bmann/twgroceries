Commercial Street Night Market

Boris Mann 25th July 2023 at 3:59pm
eventNanaimo

Evening shopping begins Thursday, June 22, in the heart of downtown Nanaimo from 5-9 p.m. and will run on every subsequent Thursday through Aug. 31. Vehicle traffic on Commercial Street will be blocked off to allow shoppers to peruse and enjoy the sights and sounds, said Aurora Zuraw, night market coordinator with Greater Nanaimo Chamber of Commerce, in an e-mail interview.

Two hundred vendors will be on Commercial selling crafts, items, food and the like, she said.
