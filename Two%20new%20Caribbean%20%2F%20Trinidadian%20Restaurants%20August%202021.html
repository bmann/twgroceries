Two new Caribbean / Trinidadian Restaurants August 2021

Boris Mann 28th August 2021 at 7:49am
Journal

We got takeout from Baby Dhal Roti, a new Trinidadian restaurant on Commercial Drive.

They were out of a number of things, so we tried goat curry, chicken curry, beef curry, and veggie curry with Barb, Roland, and Simon. Served with potatoes and rice and beans, and a little sweet mayo slaw on the side.

Really tasty, needed a bit of hot sauce on the side.

Drank the two House of Funk beers with Roland. The House of Funk Funk Juice Smoothie Sour Blackberry + Raspberry was really great. House of Funk Juno was also good, but not as strongly flavoured or tasty as the smoothie.
