Sitting in the Iceland airport KEF thinking about the last week.

I didn’t have any fermented shark. I didn’t have any puffin. I did eat a lot of tasty Icelandic lamb.

We stayed at the Exeter Hotel, which has breakfast included. Most mornings I had bread with butter and salmon, cheese, and salami.

Local Icelandic salmon for sure, but is it smoked or cured? Here’s what I found just now: When is lox not lox? When it’s smoked salmon. Ok, so it was lox.
