Hearthstone weekly bread specials

For dinner, I shredded a handful of the chicken drumsticks and picked onions, dill, thyme, onion, and red and green chard from the garden.

Picking chard and onions and herbs

Sautéed onion and garlic in butter. Then added fresh herbs and chicken, and finely chopped chard stalks. Added lemon juice, salt, and pepper, some oat milk, and finely grated parmesan and cooked it down.

Cooked the fresh linguini, drained it. Added the chicken sauce to the pot, and added in chopped chard leaves until wilted and hot. Then added some more fresh Parmesan and the noodles and mixed to coat.
