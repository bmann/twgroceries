Southern Italian Poached Fish

Boris Mann 29th August 2021 at 7:40am
Cook's Illustrated July & August 2021fishItalianRecipe

aka Pesce All'Acqua Pazza

  • 1 1/2 pounds skin-on haddock fillets
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced thin
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 small onion, chopped fine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 ounces cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 12 fresh parsley stems

Could definitely use Skipper Otto Hake for this.
