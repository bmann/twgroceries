Beaucoup Bakerymooncake

We pre-ordered mooncakes from Beacoup Bakery for the Mid-Autumn Festival they called La Petite Lune – Scout Magazine covered it.

These are smaller mooncakes, featuring brown butter sweet potato with roasted pecan marzipan; vanilla rice pudding with pistachio marzipan; and raspberry currant currant cheesecake.

The packaging was all beautiful.

These are called The Prince (mango sago pomelo) and The Fox (jasmine strawberry) and are 3.25" tarts for sharing :)
