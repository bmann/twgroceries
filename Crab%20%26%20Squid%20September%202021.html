I was inspired by this recipe for Giant Squid with Lemon Viniagrette but kind of went my own direction.

The squid was a giant jumbo squid tube, cleaned, that I cut into smaller strips. I added some Datu Puti White Vinegar Spiced and a bunch of chopped fresh basil and a little olive oil and a little Dijon mustard and let it marinate for an hour or so.

In a pan, I sauteed red onions with 3 strips of diced bacon. Once the bacon was close to crispy, I added some garlic, then added diced fresh tomatoes. I had thinly diced a whole red pepper as well. Capers and lemon juice rounded out the flavours, a little salt to taste. Added in the squid and let it simmer until cooked through.

The crab I boiled for 15 minutes in a flavoured broth. I had chucked in the broccoli stalks, the stems from a bunch of fresh cilantro, a couple of cloves of garlic, some shallots, and a sliced knob of ginger. Plus a liberal amount of salt.

I kept this broth and tried a little at the end of dinner. Salty, I brightened it with a little rice vinegar. I saved a bunch to have as just a broth or maybe to cook some veggies in.

The strawberry ice cream & dark chocolate gelato went really nicely together.
