I filtered and bottled the bay leaf + Szechuan peppercorn tepache. It’s good!

It sat for 5 days because I put it together and then went on a trip to Seattle for 2 days and then forgot about it until this morning when I had a little bit of time to deal with it.

The tepache is dry, really great taste. I think the bay leaf is coming through in a nice way as well as some floral notes from the peppercorns.

There is a light bit of spicy aftertaste but I get that usually from the ginger.

I’ve been drinking the first batch and it’s pretty acidic and sweet.
