CSA Details

CSA members purchase their share of the crop at the beginning of the season and receive 20 boxes of in-season produce (one box every week, delivered on Mondays, starting in June)

https://gloriousorganics.com/csa/

Boxes are delivered to neighbourhood drop-off locations before 3pm every Monday during the CSA season. CSA members are responsible for picking up their boxes from their selected location. Planned locations for 2022 are:

Vancouver: Commercial on 5th Vancouver: Woodland at 15th Vancouver: Kitsilano at 2nd and Maple Vancouver: Sophia & 33rd North Vancouver: Loyd Ave. at 20th Street West North Vancouver: St. Andrews at 26th Ave East Aldergrove: On the farm at 1374 256th Street
