Went to Kozak Eatery in Gastown for lunch today. Traditional Ukrainian food. Got the “Mama’s Lunch” which is perogies, sausage, cabbage roll, and a cabbage and carrot slaw.

They have a mini deli setup at the front, with pastries, bread, plus a mini pantry with pickles and mustard and frozen pierogies for sale.
