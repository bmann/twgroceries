Boris Mann 5th March 2023 at 9:14pm
Journal

Went to Deep Cove to go to the Seymour Art Gallery. Walked around the cove, which has just started more construction. Windy but sunny.

Went to Cafe Orso and Honey’s Doughnuts.

Came home and took Skipper Otto Halibut out of the freezer. Used the rest of the jumbo carrot sliced into rounds and layered in a baking dish with a hefty amount of olive oil. Sliced 4 cloves garlic, 2 onions. Salt, pepper, marjoram. Sliced 5 Roma tomatoes, and added salt, pepper, marjoram, and basil. 2 Tbsp capers and a Tbsp of caper brine.

Put the big halibut filet on top and covered in aluminum foil. The fish will defrost and we’ll make for dinner.
