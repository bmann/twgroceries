I hadn’t eaten lunch so ended up going to Babylon Cafe, the shawarma place on The Drive. I hadn’t been there in a long time. It wasn’t good. I don’t really have a go to shawarma place in Vancouver anymore: there was a Babylon on Robson that was my go to long ago.

Got my hair cut and then met Andy at Mum’s the Word. It’s a cafe during the day and a bar at night.

The coffee they serve is either pour over of various kinds. I had the priciest one, an anaerobic one, and it was good. They don’t serve anything with cream or milk — and it didn’t need it.

Then had a whiskey sour and Andy had an Old Fashioned. All the cocktails are 3oz.

The decor is all used furniture and like 70s rec room. Cozy.
