Boris Mann 5th March 2023 at 9:06pm
Journal

No bacon, so I sautéed some King Oyster Mushrooms along with an egg omelette.

Went to the Burnaby Art Gallery, then up to Burnaby Heights for grocery shopping. Went to Ayoub’s and got some nuts and Persian jam cookies.

Then Sungiven, then Cioffi’s.

I made a pork stew from a Berkshire pork hock from Sungiven, plus a jumbo carrot, gai lan, coconut milk, onions, garlic, fish sauce, soy sauce. Browned the onions and hocks and then added liquids and veggies and braised for 3 hours. Added peeled and diced Russet potatoes for the last hour.
