First day of our vacation, heading down to Orcas Island to visit Lee & Sachi.

Rented a car from ACE Car Rental, which has a kiosk in the lobby of the Delta Hotel on W Hastings.

Border only took about 20 minutes or so, and then we drove to Bellingham and did our first grocery tourism at the Trader Joes there.

Several interesting vinegars. I was sort of thinking about making shrubs.

  • Trader Joes White Modena Vinegar
  • Agrodolce White Wine Vinegar with Grape Must

Huh, agrodolce is interesting

Bought a variety pack of beer and liquor:

  • Ecliptic Brewing Carina Peach Sour
  • Gray Whale Gin
  • Trader Joes Fino Sherry

A few other snacks and staples:

  • Rolled Corn Tortilla Chips - Chili & Lime

We ended up driving to Rosario Beach for a picnic lunch and it was a lovely spot and it was amazingly sunny.

We still had lots of time to wait at the Anacortes Ferry Terminal, so we went along the shore walk there. I now know the difference between sword ferns and bracken ferns.

Arriving at Flattop, we went right into the garden and picked out a bunch of cilantro and parsley and chives to make schug.

And picked chive flowers and put them in a jar with the Trader Joes White Modena Vinegar.

Sachi made crab cakes with an awesome salad. Yes, schug tastes good with crab cakes :)

Lee made a very tasty cocktail with the Gray Whale gin – lime, hint of serrano chile.
