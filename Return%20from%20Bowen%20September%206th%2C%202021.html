Return from Bowen September 6th, 2021

Boris Mann 6th September 2021 at 10:00pm
JournalLabour Day

We stayed on Bowen Island for a week, from last Sunday to Saturday.

Yesterday we went to the Nikkei Museum.

Today is a lazy Labour Day holiday Monday. I got up at my usual time just after 6am, because I had a call with someone in Japan, and then we just got on with our day.

Rachael and I walked over to Hastings Sunrise. Laughing Bean was closed for the holiday, so we went in to Platform 7 and sat in their nice little back garden for a cappucino and an oat milk chai latte. This is one of the places that makes a good chai latte – nice spice level, not too milky, not too sweet.

We picked up a few household things at London Drugs and some other staples at Donalds.

Now I've installed the Latest version of the file uploads plugin and so far haven't uploaded any images but instead written a ton of wiki pages to cover various events :)
