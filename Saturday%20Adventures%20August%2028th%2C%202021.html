Saturday Adventures August 28th, 2021

Boris Mann 29th August 2021 at 5:41am
Journal

Tomorrow we're going to Bowen Island for the week, today we wandered all over town.

We started by walking into Chinatown to go to a pottery sale at Hand Eye Design Ceramics.

This is not at all what we purchased, but I thought it was really well done. A series of "donut" ceramics:

An interesting display:

Rachael bought a the hanging plant pot in the promo poster, and I bought a small mortar and pestle.

We kept walking through Chinatown and saw some melons:

Will have to figure out what they are.

Right next door, paused to look in at the Irish Heather at its new location and promised Sean to send a horde along soon.

Kouign Cafe was just opening, and we ended up being their very first dine in customers. Here's their front window display:

We ordered a drink and a pastry each. I had the Vietnamese iced coffee with condensed milk, and Rachael had Golden Fog: mango and passionfruit earl grey tea, cold with condensed milk.

I always like Vietnamese coffee, but Rachael's drink was luscious. The mango and the condensed milk together made it really smooth and creamy.

Now a close up of those pastries:

I had the "Treasure Chest" kouign amman:

our classic Kouign Amann with caramelized sugar crust, savoury miso gravy custard, shiitake mushrooms, truffled potatoes, winter herbs, scallions, chrysanthemum honey glaze, maldon sea salt, and 24K gold

The truffle taste was a little strong for me, but the savoury and sweet went really well together.

And Rachael had the "Nutcracker" financier:

brown butter, toasted almond, and white sesame earl grey infused financier with ivory white chocolate ganache heart

Then we went off to Kits because Rachael wanted to check out branches & knots, a Japanese clothing and lifestyle store.

On the way home, the strange red fruit of a dogwood tree caught my eye:

We ate a late lunch at home, and then took our big chairs out to Woodland Park and sat in the sun. A good Saturday.
