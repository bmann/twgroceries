August 6th, 2022

Boris Mann 8th August 2022 at 5:22am
Journal

Went to Coho Cafe and had a dulce de leche filled cruffin made by Heritage Baking, along with a cappuccino and an iced oat chai.

Then went to Eastside Atelier on Williams and toured some First Saturday Open Studios.

For lunch, walked up and went to Community Pizza for the first time. They have New York style and “Detroit Style” pizzas.

We got two “mini” Detroit Style pizzas and a Caesar salad to share.

I got a beer flight:

  • Wildeye French Wheat ale
  • Audina Peach Guava (this was terrible and I didn’t drink it)
  • Moody Flamingose
  • North Point Sour

The server was great! We ended up talking about gardening and he said he’s just starting to get tomatoes.

Santa Barbara didn’t have much produce (worrying! never seen it so bare!) so we went up to Norman's Fruit and Salad and got a few things.

Norman’s Shopping Trip August 6th, 2022

  • Okanagan hot peppers
  • pickling cucumbers
  • Krino’s Roasted Yellow Peppers
  • plums
  • cherries

I’m not sure how long Norman’s has been under new ownership. There was a sale on shelved items and the produce is not as good — or as plentiful — as it used to be.
