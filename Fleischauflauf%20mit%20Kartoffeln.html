From the Spezialitäten Aus Aller Welt cookbook.

This is a Dutch recipe, known as Filosoof, aka "Philosopher's Stew".

The English translation of the German name would be "Meat Casserole with Potatoes".

Here are the translated ingredients.

  • 6 Potatoes
  • 1/2 Cup Cream
  • 125g Butter
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 3 cups ground beef
  • 3/4 cups beef bouillon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • pinch of cloves
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs OR 1/2 cup of grated cheese
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 bunch of chervil
  • 2 leafs of lovage
  • 1 twig of fresh rosemary
  • 1 twig of fresh marjoram
