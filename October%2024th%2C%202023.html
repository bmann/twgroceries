Went for lunch with Rachael at Truck Stop Cafe.

It was packed at noon but we did wait a bit and get a table.

Rachael got her usual classic burger. I got pictures of the daily specials menu and decided to finally try one of their Chinese food items. Tuesday special is Sweet and Sour Pork with chow mein and fried rice. AND soup of the day!

Pretty sour the breaded sweet and sour pork balls were house made.
