Lee’s Donuts is a mom and pop donut shop located in the Granville Island Public Market in Vancouver, B.C. We have been serving handmade, quality, classic donuts based on original recipes developed by founders Alan and Betty-Ann Lee in 1979. Lee’s Donuts is committed to providing a fast, friendly and familiar customer experience and serving donuts made daily from scratch using only fresh ingredients.
