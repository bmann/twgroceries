Picked up pastries and coffee at Oide Coffee and went to meet Rachael at 1000 Parker. It was Parker Arts Salon so we wandered around the building looking at art.

Then we went downtown to Japan Market happening in the square in front of the Vancouver Art Gallery.

We tried a “hero” from Kyu Grill, a Hawaiian Japanese fusion food truck:

Soft sweet Hawaiian bun, pork belly, a little slaw underneath. Battered fries and a drink on the side.

It was Ok. Messy to eat.
