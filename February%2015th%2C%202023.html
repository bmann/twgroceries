Catching up on my travels.

I’m in Toronto this week. Staying at a Sonder in Liberty Village.

Today I’ll tour Paradise Theatre, Lula Lounge and Halo Brewery.

Grabbed breakfast at Liberty Village Market & Cafe. I thought I might sit down to some bacon & eggs but it was a bit chaotic. Had a toasted sandwich with microwaved eggs and cheese and avocado. A giant cappuccino in a takeout cup.

Met James at the Paradise and then grabbed a quick Italian sandwich and a coffee from Cafe Paradise. All of the pastries and other baked goods looked really good. They will be catering breakfast for Causal Islands.
