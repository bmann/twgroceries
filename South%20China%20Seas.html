Founded in 1994, South China Seas Trading Co sells the highest quality spices and exotic ingredients from around the world. Located in the Granville Island Public Market in Vancouver, South China Seas has been a central destination for locals, tourists and foodies from around the world.

Our spices and proprietary blends are made in house using a custom mix of herbs and spices from our growing partners around the world. We pride ourselves on our sustainably grown and ethically sourced ingredients sourced from growing partners around the world.

Our product offering continues to grow as we uncover unique spices, sauces and ingredients from around the globe.
