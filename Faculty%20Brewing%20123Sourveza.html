MEXICAN LIME SOUR

This is a fusion of styles: inspired by both a classic Mexican light lager, and a citrus-forward kettle sour, this beer was fermented with kveik yeast and conditioned on lime zest and lime juice. Expect aromas of citrus fruit and banana, a tart lemon-lime punch up front, and a crispy clean lager-like finish.

Alcohol Content: 4.3 ABV

Bitterness: 5 IBU
