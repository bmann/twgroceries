Opened in 2002 in the heart of Little Italy, Dolce Amore has grown from a local scoop shop to an artisanal crafter of Vancouver's first and only Grass-Fed gelato. Daniela (Grippo) Cusano has grown up producing gelato since the age of 14, and now along with her husband Giancarlo, these Carpigiani Gelato University Graduates look to welcome you in to savour their ever-changing gelato creations. To us, gelato means family, and we welcome you in to our Gelato Mafia Family with open arms.
