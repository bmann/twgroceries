Beef Stew January 2024

  • beef stew 1” cubes
  • 6 onions sliced
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • salt & lots of fresh black pepper

Cooked on low broil to get onions browned and meat seared, mixing as we go.

Then:

  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • large jar chopped roasted red pepper
  • 3 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp Calabrian chillies

Low and slow at 350F as onions melt and beef gets tender.
