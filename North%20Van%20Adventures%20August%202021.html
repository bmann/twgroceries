North Van Adventures August 2021

Boris Mann 22nd August 2021 at 5:41pm
Journal

Our plan was to go see a show at the Seymour Art Gallery in Deep Cove. Turns out there is a construction project to replace the sewers and so there are massive detours everywhere and nowhere to park.

We drove down to the waterfront, dropping off the Evo by House of Funk, which is a craft beer and coffee roaster. I tried their bourbon barrel aged beans at Revolver during the week.

I got a coffee to go and picked up a couple of single beer cans — House of Funk Juno & House of Funk Funk Juice Smoothie Sour Blackberry + Raspberry.

(images obviously taken at home at the end of the day)

No chai latte for Rachael, so we walked over to the Quay and got her one from Shipyards Coffee.

Then we walked along the Spirit Trail ending behind Capilano Mall. Did a quick visit to the Opus Art Supplies and then drove up to Bob’s Fruit Stand.

I didn't buy any of the garlic or crab apples, just sort of an example of some of the stuff they have and how it is priced.

They had a ton of field tomatoes from Keremeos. I got some ripe red ones as well as a handful of green tomatoes.

The real stand out were perfectly ripe raspberries, which Rachael started snacking on in the car. Also got some Original Garlic Scape Salt.

Since we were in North Van with an Evo, one more adventure stop: Laleh Bakery for koolocheh — Persian cookies!
