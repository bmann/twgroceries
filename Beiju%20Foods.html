Works out of Coho Commissary. Cassava based breads and flours.

Beijú Foods is a Canadian company proudly founded in BC by Brazilian immigrants to honor their roots and promote the best of Brazil’s vibrant food culture. Made from 100% cassava roots harvested, peeled, and ground to perfection creating the signature flavor used in a myriad of recipes. Our mission is to bring new and sustainable food choices to your daily life so you can enjoy a healthier diet with exotic new flavors...
