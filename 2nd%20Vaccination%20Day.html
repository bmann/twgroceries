2nd Vaccination Day

Boris Mann 27th June 2021 at 7:50am
Journal

Today Rachael and I got our second vaccinations. We went to VCC off Great Northern Way.

But first, in the morning we drove over to the Museum of Vancouver to drop off some of Rachael's artwork which is in a pop-up that's happening there.

I took a picture of The Crab:

On the way back, we stopped at Oh Carolina, a cafe and mini grocery. Rachael got an iced matcha latte and I got a cappucino, plus a croissant to share. All were very good, looking forward to trying other things there.

Oh Carolina is a throwback to a time gone by. The store on the corner that becomes a second home. Where friends, families, and neighbours meet for coffee, breakfast, and lunch.

We’ve consciously sourced a selection of local & international groceries for your enjoyment. Pick up your staples, high quality meats, frozen foods, dry goods, sweet treats and local farm fresh vegetables.

Have us over to your friends, take us for a picnic, swing by on the way home. We can’t wait to serve you, Mount Pleasant.

Part of Gooseneck Hospitality

Also picked up some BC Brine Pickled Beets. We opened them for lunch, and they weren't that great. Nothing interesting flavour wise, other than a sharp edge to the vinegar they used.

Capturing a Friday shopping trip, we went to Welk's and stocked up on Johnny's Pops, as well as got some interesting drink syrups.

And then did a bigger shop at Famous Foods.

We just realized today that apparently we left some of this stuff in the Evo car we used? Going to figure out if it got handed in to lost and found.
