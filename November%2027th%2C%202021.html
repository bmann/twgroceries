I got a request for some recommendations of local restaurants that would have gift certificates, somewhere on the west side.

Since we're way over in East Van, I don't have a lot of recommendations over there off the top of my head.

I took a quick look and came up with three promising ones, that are within a couple of blocks of each other on West 4th:

  • Maenam - upscale Thai food, really good. https://maenam.ca/
  • Raisu - a new to me up scale Japanese place that looks really good. On my to try list! http://www.raisu.ca/
  • Delara Restaurant - a Persian restaurant. Vancouver has a very large population of people with Persian backgrounds, many of them in North Vancouver. This restaurant is new to me, also going on the Want list. https://delararestaurant.ca/

And here are some other places that aren't on the west side at all. The central City of Vancouver isn't really very big, so it's easy to go to any of these places:

Savio Volpe is always great. Family style italian with a wood fired grill.

Kissa Tanto is a high end, special meals favourite. Does Japanese / Italian sound weird? I promise you it's amazing.

We're off to try Kin Kao Song tonight for the first time. The sister restaurant Kin Kao is right up the street from us.
