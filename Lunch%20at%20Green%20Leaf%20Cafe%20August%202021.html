Lunch at Green Leaf Cafe August 2021

Boris Mann 21st August 2021 at 9:20am
Journal

JeffG and I went for lunch to the Green Leaf Cafe to catch up with Ben.

A really great Japanese place with lots of oshi, and aburi oshi.

They were out of a lot of raw items, and also didn't have their fryer working for lunch.

Mentaiko Creamy Linguini Udon

Not pictured: a second order of udon with Beef Yaki Udon, without cream, for JeffG who has dairy issues.

Tornado Omurice

This tornado version is impressive looking, but apparently the non-tornado version is the same, just cheaper.

I like the Dosanko omurice version with tomato sauce better.

Aburi Oshi

A whole selection. Apparently aburi means flame torched and oshi means pressed. They were out of their "fresh" oshi, which I'd like to try as well.
