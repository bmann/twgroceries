This was on the back cover of the Cook’s Illustrated September & October 2021. I should make a habit of posting these back covers, they have fun lists of things which make for both good prompts, and an excuse to get some more food terms into the system.

  • Bialys - new to me
  • Pumpernickel
  • Jewish Rye
  • Bagels - have never made them at home, I should try
  • Laffa - new to me
  • Kubaneh
  • Babka - Coho Cafe has this regularly, include an Ube babka version
