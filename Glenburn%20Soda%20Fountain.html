Glenburn Soda Fountain

Boris Mann 6th April 2023 at 2:11am
BurnabyBurnaby HeightsRestaurant

Glenburn Soda Fountain opened in March 2013 in Burnaby Heights. Owned by Ron and Roberta LaQuaglia, area residents, Glenburn is a throw-back to a simpler time and an opportunity to enjoy the simple pleasures of a hand-made dessert. Glenburn offers a wide range of ice cream treats including sundaes, milkshakes and malts. Candy and soda syrups along with nostalgic toys and treats round out the offerings.
