August 13th, 2022

Boris Mann 14th August 2022 at 5:34am
Journal

Cappuccino and an iced oat milk chai latte from Coho Cafe plus pain au chocolat and a Tall Shadow Breads blue cheese bun.

Drove out to Surrey Art Gallery and then walked around Bear Creek Park for a bit. Then spent entirely too much time in Guildford Town Centre mall.

We didn’t get any samosas, but liked the look of Golden Samosa Bakery.

A lovely swim in the evening. Corn, steamed green beans, and European wieners for dinner, with a slice of bread and the last of the Appenzeller Classic cheese.

Before going to bed, put a pork picnic shoulder on to sous vide overnight. Made a marinade of fresh ginger, dry minced garlic, bay lead, miso, soy sauce, black pepper, and sugar.
