Bike Rides

Boris Mann 27th June 2021 at 4:56am
Journal

I used the Flourist Sourdough Pan Loaf to make a lovely breakfast sandwich – eggs, red onions, gouda scrambled together and a little mayo underneath.

We biked out to Jericho Beach and sat in the sun for a while, and then rode back along the off broadway and then 10th Ave bike routes.

On the way back, stopped at the Federal Store for a Johnny's Pops (Mango Coconut flavour) for Rachael, and a cappucino for me.

For dinner, used up the Preferisco Fregola to make a one dish meal – plus a little tomato, cucumber, and olive side salad.

Sliced a yellow onion and sautéed in olive and butter with a little salt and pepper. Added in one diced garlic clove, head of broccoli cut into chunks, diced orange bell pepper, some shelled English peas. Added 2 Tbsp of pesto, juice from a fresh lemon.

After dinner, cut up all the rhubarb with some cane sugar to make a rhubarb syrup and rhubarb shrub.
