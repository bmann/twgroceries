Skipper Otto is a Community Supported Fishery CSF. Like the more common Community Support Agriculture CSA, you buy membership shares in the form of dollar credits at the start of the season, and then order and pick up as different fish and other seafood come in.

Available Products

  • Skipper Otto Hake
  • Skipper Otto Halibut
  • Skipper Otto Lingcod
  • Skipper Otto Octopus
  • Skipper Otto Wild Pink Salmon
  • Wild Pink & Spiny Scallops

    • Pickup Locations

    Metro Vancouver

    Also available in other locations, frozen and shipped.
