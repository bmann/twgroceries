Sunday June 13th, 2021

Boris Mann 14th June 2021 at 3:54am
JournalShopping Trip

Today was an extremely rainy day so we hung out inside mostly.

I entered lots of the Cook's Illustrated July & August 2021 recipes into the site here.

I made the Lemonade Concentrate recipe and it came out really nice. Muddling the lemon zest in sugar really brings out a nice flavour.

In the afternoon we went for a short walk in the rain and stopped in at Norman's Fruit and Salad and picked up a few things:

  • frozen samosas
  • baby cucumbers
  • broccoli
  • local beefsteak tomatoes
  • zucchini
  • strawberries

The samosa were pretty good! I baked them at 375F for like 40min, and then we used the included tamarind chutney for dipping. We'll have to pick up some more tamarind sauce, we used it up on our 4 mini samosas each.

I used one of the zucchini to make "zoodles" – aka Zucchini Noodles with Soy & Sesame.

And at the end of the day I bottled some tepache I had started during the week.
