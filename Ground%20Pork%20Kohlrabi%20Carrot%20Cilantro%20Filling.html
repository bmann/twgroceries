Ground Pork Kohlrabi Carrot Cilantro Filling

Boris Mann 11th September 2021 at 5:52am
Meal Prep

I thought I might do a play by play of cooking, but only have some sauteeing onions and ginger:

And then the final filling:

So, sauteed a diced onion plus a 2 inch knob of ginger in some oil. Added 1.5 lbs of ground pork.

I peeled and finely diced a kohlrabi and two large carrots. I diced two large tomatoes. I minced the stalks from a large bunch of cilantro.

I put in some of the chilli garlic sauce I bought, as well as 2 tsp of Hikari Miso Savoury White Miso.
