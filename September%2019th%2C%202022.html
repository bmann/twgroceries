BerlinJournal

My first 9 nights in Berlin are over. Now I’m hanging out with Dad for this week and taking a bit of vacation time.

I moved from art’Otel Berlin Mitte to TWO Hotel by Axel, which is around the corner from where my parents are staying.

Naturbäckerei Czerr Konditorei is the little bakery and pastry shop around the corner, listed as being opened in 1948. Felt very traditional Berlin style.

Went back to Kreuzberg to hang out with Rosano. He took me to his favourite little coffee & pastries place Katie’s Blue Cat. Coffee beans by Five Elephants.

Went to Anh Ba for dinner. Had a nice chat with the server (in German!) about types of pho, and mentioned pho dac biet. They have to keep it pretty plain to serve the local customers.
