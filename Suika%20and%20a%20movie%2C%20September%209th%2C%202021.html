Suika and a movie, September 9th, 2021

Boris Mann 11th September 2021 at 5:30am
Journal

We went to Suika before going to see Shang-Chi.

Chilled Savory Duck Udon

Wasamayo Brullop Fritter (special) - tempura battered scallops and brussel sprouts with a wasabi mayo dipping sauce.

Suika Aburi Hamachi Carpaccio

Yellowtail seared with mayonnaise, Shiitake mushroom and parmesan. Served with spicy sesame soy dressing.

I ordered Cucumber Q Chan which they initially didn't bring but I asked about it. It's actually not a salad, but actually tsukemono – Japanese pickles made with soy. I tried to make this at home and didn't like my version either!

For dessert, a really tasty Velvety Almond Tofu with Passionfruit and Mango Sauce.

The duck udon, fritters, and tofu were all from the daily specials menu.

I wanted to order the Orange & Grapefruit Gin Tonic from the drink specials, but they were out, so just had an Asahi Beer.
