Went for lunch to Red Wagon Cafe for lunch with Rachael. First time I’ve eaten at this new location. Rachael got the Cobb Salad, which is a huge salad with fried chicken on top.

I got the Briskett breakfast meal — brisket, two eggs, BBQ sauce, on jalapeño cornbread. It’s fine. The brisket is really good, but I don’t really like their tomato based BBQ sauce.

And then another meal: MILA for a Farcaster Dinner. Got the aburi with “carrot” lox, and had the Dan Dan noodles as my main. After the Rain cocktail.
