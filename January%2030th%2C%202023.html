Heard that Santa Barbara is closing. Or rather, got bought and then will re-open likely with a new name.

From a sign on a telephone pole:

Yes, it will be reopening, but the new store will allegedly have a new name and new staff. Most, if not all, of the current staff will be leaving.

Went to Choices for a shop: bananas, oat milk, eggs, English muffins, toilet paper, and a bag of mandarin oranges. The mandarins are nice and sweet.

I made mashed potatoes, pork sausages, and broccoli for dinner. We have these russet potatoes instead of our usual Yukon gold, so I peeled the potatoes. I decided to use the peels to make something, and also had a quarter head of cabbage that needed using up.

So I ended up with a sort of okonomiyaki / latke cross. Potato peels that I chopped a bit finer, chopped fine cabbage, salt, pepper, paprika, ancho chile powder, 2 eggs, flour, a little water. Made two pancakes in the cast iron pan. I had half of one for dinner, will have the other half with eggs for breakfast.
