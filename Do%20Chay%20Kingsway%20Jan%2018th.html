Truffle Dumplings and Coconut Rice Cakes from last time.

Spring Rolls — Crispy vegan spring rolls; mustard greens and herbs for wrapping; ish sauce

Served with herbs and lettuce leaves for wrapping. Tasty!

Desert Island Noodles — Thick noodles, coconut milk, vegan meatball, tomato, shredded tofu mix, peanut, ish sauce, greens and herbs

Tasty and some crunch. But maybe a little flat.

Vegan Prawn Curry — Plant-based prawns, eggplant, carrot, potato coconut curry, daikon, tofu, sweet potato crisp

The prawns are made of gluten, dusted with paprika for “prawn stripes”. The curry wasn’t very strong. Don’t think we’ll order this again.
