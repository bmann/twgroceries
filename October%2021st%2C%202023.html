Stopped in at Mah Milk Bar on Victoria Drive, which just opened a week or so ago. We got in just before a long line formed.

Rachael says the oak milk chai is very good. Which is rare! My cappuccino was good. We bought some Schocki Chocolate, they’re also a little corner grocery. I looked at some fun House of Funk Coffee Bean packaging.

Bought a few things at Columbus Meat Market including some rolled Italian meat loaf, stuffed with arugula, cheese, and pancetta.
