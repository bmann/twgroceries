First farm sales on Westham Island, on your right as you drive onto the island.

My farm is open to the public from the first Saturday in May to October 31 and offers an ever changing smorgasbord of goodies. Bring your mother out for Mother's Day, the family out for berry picking or your school class out for a visit to the pumpkin patch. Be sure to attend our "Day at the Farm" in September to learn about the importance of local farmland and the products it provides.
