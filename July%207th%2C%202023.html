Went to watch the new Spiderverse movie tonight at Fifth Avenue. Grabbed dinner ahead of time just up the street at Unchai (which apparently Kenny recommended back in January).

We sat outside and got three dishes.

Som Tam, Thai Green Papaya Salad. Somewhat obscured by too much salad leaves when I took the picture.

Roti with curry dip. A little but puffed up and perfectly crispy. They also have it on the menu with condensed milk.

Plus a Thai iced coffee that was tasty.

Khao Pad Moo Tod

Fried rice with deep fried pork jowl, green onion, coriander, egg, cherry tomatoes.

And also gai lan.
