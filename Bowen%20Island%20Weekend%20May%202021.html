Bowen Island Weekend May 2021

Boris Mann 6th June 2021 at 10:05pm
Bowen IslandJournal

My mom made a “deconstructed” shepherd’s pie (as Rachael calls it) when we arrived on Friday. This is technically a Dutch dish from a German cookbook: Fleischauflauf mit Kartoffeln (Meat Casserole with Potatoes), from Spezialitäten Aus Aller Welt (Specialities from Around the World).

As you can tell from the stains, this cookbook is well used. My mom bought it back when she was living in Berlin in the early 70s.

On Saturday, I went to the Bowen Beer & Wine Cellar with my dad and bought a variety pack of beers. He likes pretty straightforward pilsners plus a few other traditional German styles. I of course wanted to try some weird stuff.

  • 2x Hacker Pschorr Weißbier
  • 2x Howe Sound Brewing Pilsner
  • Phillips Blue Buck
  • Phillips Dino Sour Blackberry Sour Ale
  • Phillips Space Goat Juicy IPA
  • Persephone Peach Hefeweizen

Made some cast iron pan steak for dinner. On the side, some baked cauliflower with cumin, and then a cannelini bean salad with garlic, carrots, and olives.
