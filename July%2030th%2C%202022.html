Went to the Powell Street Festival.

Artisan Sake Maker was there serving amazake with ice and flavours. We got the lemon yuzu one. They make the amazake from the leftover rice from the sake making process.

Had some squid yaki ika from the Teriyaki Boys Food Truck and a braised garlic eggplant and tofu with rice cans cabbage.
