I made a German potato salad with some elderly red potatoes, green onion, minced dill pickles, pickle juice, diced red bell pepper, Dijon mustard, and olive oil. Used quite a lot of mustard, we’ll see how people like it.

And a spicy basil garlic sumac pasta salad. I made the dressing the blender: one bunch fresh basil, 3 green onions, 6 Tbsp olive oil, 3 Tbsp red wine vinegar, 3 cloves garlic, 1 Tbsp sumac, 1/2 Tbsp salt, 1 fresh hot banana pepper including seeds. Blended until smooth and fine, then stirred into some fusilli noodles.
