Took a little time to cook myself lunch with some leftovers. A sort of fake carbonara.

Had the last of some bacon ends that I cut up and sautéed until browning. Added 1 onion, sliced thin. 3 cloves of garlic. A little butter with the garlic. Black pepper. Everything nicely browned.

Cooked and drained Sungiven flat noodles and reserved a cup of pasta water. Mixed in with bacon, onions, garlic mixture on low heat.

Squeezed in juice of half a fresh lemon.

Whisked 2 eggs along with Bulgarian feta cheese with a little brine. Poured it over noodles and mixed. Added some reserved pasta water.

Garnished with thinly sliced Serrano peppers.
