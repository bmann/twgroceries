Went to Valoroso Foods on Clark Drive. It’s an Italian imports store, both open to the public and for wholesale.

The owners are apparently Sicilian, so many of the products are imported from Sicily.

We bought a few things to try them out, after doing a mini tasting of some of the vinegars and olive oils.

  • Condimente Nicol White Balsamic, somewhat similar to Trader Joes Agrodolce
  • Coppini Arte Olearia Fruttato Medio Olive Oil
  • pasta Fabbrica della Pasta di Gragnano IGP Straccetti
  • Lucifora Ready Sauce with Cherry Tomato in a stubby bottle
  • Murgella Burrata
  • La Mole It’s Foccacia Garlic and Basil crackers

They have a deli case with cold cuts and whole wheels of cheese.
