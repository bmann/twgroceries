White Sails Lost Lake Pale Ale

TASTING NOTES:

A crisp, clean and crushable Northwest Pale Ale, light in body and moderately hopped with a blend of Vic Secret and Mosaic hops for a bright, hop forward profile with notes of citrus and pineapple with a restrained bitterness.

INGREDIENTS: Water, malted barley, hops, yeast

FEATURED HOPS: Vic Secret and Mosaic STYLE: Northwest Pale Ale

SPECS: 5.0% abv
