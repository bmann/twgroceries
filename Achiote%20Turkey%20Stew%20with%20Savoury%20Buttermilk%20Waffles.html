Achiote Turkey Stew with Savoury Buttermilk Waffles

Boris Mann 11th October 2023 at 4:00am
achioteAllTheBestRecipes

I bought a couple of turkey thighs and with sort of a black bean and cumin Mexican stew in mind.

Going through some old supplies I found I still had some achiote that I bought the last time I was in Mexico:

[image|373x500](upload:ivgphXG562EcRCV7oTuLxHaMa0Q.jpeg) The instructions say this is enough for 2kg of meat, so I used half of it. I cut the turkey thighs up and squeezed out the juice of a whole red grapefruit, and added some olive oil, and some salt and pepper. ![image|500x500](upload:wTb94wxHuBdu12gYzaqEbUOQPtf.jpeg)

I broiled an onion and some celery root along with the two turkey thigh bones.

[image|500x500](upload:xLKG9EQNuRtB0aKzrKqM1dRPHrs.jpeg) I took the turkey out of the marinade and sautéed in oil and garlic and then added back the marinating liquid, a can of black beans, and the onion + celery root mixture. ![image|500x500](upload:tsYFsgQFu6qu13wdt1FoqZp2rYd.jpeg)

I made savoury buttermilk waffles by using the [buttermilk waffle recipe](https://allthebest.recipes/t/buttermilk-waffles/470), but one less tablespoon of sugar, extra teaspoon of salt, and some thyme and marjoram.

I also made some plantain chips as well.

[image|492x500](upload:qCuijbqXDvB0dAHKvM9CSotP0s7.jpeg) I really like how this turned out. There could be at least twice as many black beans. There was a bit of brightness and sweetness missing. ![image|375x500](upload:8HH2lhOhGlB90Pg4UxGyreXTtax.jpeg)
