JournalWeekend Adventure

Trying out Bisou Bake House on Bidwell just off Davie in the West End.

Lots of really nice looking pastries and breads. There were some pretzel creations on the wall we’ll have to come back for.

Actually eating the pastries, they were just OK, tasting a little dry and stale. I got something that looked like a kouign amman with one side dipped in chocolate and crushed pistachios. Rachael got a savoury pasty with tomato and feta.

We walked around the sea wall a bit in the gorgeous sunshine, and then drove to the Richmond Art Gallery.

Headed to Michaels which also has a Sungiven in the same space, so we picked up a bunch of things:

  • eggs
  • Sungiven flat noodles
  • 2x frozen green onion pancakes
  • frozen pork gyoza
  • baby bok choy
  • bacon
  • cream
  • broccoli crowns
