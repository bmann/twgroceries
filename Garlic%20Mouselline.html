Garlic Mouselline

aka Mussolina d'All
Boris Mann 25th September 2021 at 5:55pm
CatalanCatalan Cuisine

I often make this with raw garlic (although less than 12 cloves!), and usually just use 2 whole eggs.

  • 12 cloves garlic, roasted
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 egg yolks, or 1 egg yolk and 1 whole egg
  • 250ml / 8 fl oz mild extra-virgin olive oil

Chick Peas with Garlic Mouselline is the thing I most often make with this, but anything that you want to add some protein and tang to would be great with this – you just run it under the broiler and it puffs up and browns.
