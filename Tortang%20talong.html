Tortang talong

aka eggplant omelette, tortalong
Boris Mann 28th July 2023 at 5:59pm
eggplantFilipinoomelette

also known as eggplant omelette, is an omelette or fritter from Filipino cuisine made by pan-frying grilled whole eggplants dipped in an egg mixture. It is a popular breakfast and lunch meal in the Philippines. A common variant of tortang talong is rellenong talong, which is stuffed with meat, seafood, and/or vegetables.
