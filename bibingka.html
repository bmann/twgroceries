Wikipedia:

Bibingka (/bɪˈbiːŋkɑː/; bi-BEENG-kah) is a type of baked rice cake from the Philippines. It is usually eaten for breakfast, especially during the Christmas season. It is traditionally cooked in clay pots lined with leaves. It is a subtype of kakanin (rice cakes) in Philippine cuisine. Bibingka is also found in East Timor and Christian communities in eastern Indonesia.
